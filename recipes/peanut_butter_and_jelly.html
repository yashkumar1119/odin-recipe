Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Description

The simplest of sandwiches that has been a childhood staple for most.

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Grape or Strawberry Jelly

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients
  2. Add Peanut Butter on one slice of bread of choice of Jelly on the other
  3. Put the breads together like a sandwich and serve!
